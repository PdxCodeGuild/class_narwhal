Arnold Schwarzenegger

Introduction

Arnold Schwarzenegger has had an inspiring and successful life since he first came to America. Arnold is widely recognized as 'The King" in the bodybuilding world due to his unique physique and for winning the Mr. Olympia title seven years in a row. This would ultimately help him grow into his next career as an actor. He became famous in Hollywood and well known for his roles in The Terminator movie series and Conan The Barbarian. In addition to his success in acting and bodybuilding, Arnold would become successful as a businessman and politician later in his life.
