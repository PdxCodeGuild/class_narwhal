Santa Clause

Santa Clause is character who is known for his jolly personality, red velvet suit, with white fur. By touching his finger to his nose, he is able to travel up and down chimneys around the world According to stories, he is known to bring toys for children who have behaved and coal for those who have misbehaved. Santa comes from the North Pole where it is said he has many small elves building toys to be delivered on Christmas. He delivers these toys around the world via his sleigh pulled by 8 flying reigndeer.
