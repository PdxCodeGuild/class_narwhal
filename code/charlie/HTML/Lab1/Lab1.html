A Brief look into the life of Stevie Ray Vaughan

Introduction:

Stevie Ray Vaughan was and is one of the premier blues guitarist of the Texas Blues style to this day. Even after his passing over 30 years ago professional and amateurs alike still try to emulate his incendiary style.

Has lived in:

Austin, Texas
Wisconsin
East Troy, Texas
Dallas, Texas
